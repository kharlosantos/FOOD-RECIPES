Egg Fried Bread

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tbsp milk (optional, for fluffiness)
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  • ½ tsp butter or oil
  • ¼ tsp chili flakes (optional)
  • ¼ tsp garlic powder (optional, for extra flavor)

Instructions:

1. In a bowl, whisk the egg, milk, salt, pepper, and any optional seasonings.
2. Heat a pan over medium heat and add butter or oil.
3. Dip the bread slices into the egg mixture, coating both sides.
4. Place the soaked bread on the pan and cook for about 2 minutes on each side until golden brown.
5. Serve hot with ketchup, chutney, or a side of fresh salad.1.

Homemade Popcorn

Ingredients:

  • ½ cup popcorn kernels
  • 2 tbsp oil (vegetable, coconut, or butter)
  • ½ tsp salt (adjust to taste)
  • 1 tbsp melted butter, cheese powder, or caramel for extra flavor(optional)

Instructions:

1. Heat oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add 2-3 kernels and cover the pot.
2. Once they pop, add the rest of the popcorn kernels in an even layer.
3. Cover the pot and shake it gently every few seconds to prevent burning.
4. When popping slows down (about 2-3 seconds between pops), remove from heat.
5. Pour the popcorn into a bowl, sprinkle with salt, and mix well.
6. Optional: Drizzle melted butter or add cheese/caramel for extra flavor.

Fried Rice

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup cooked rice (preferably cold)
  • 1 tbsp oil (vegetable or sesame)
  • ½ cup mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, corn, etc.)
  • 1 egg (optional)
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • ½ tsp salt (adjust to taste)
  • ¼ tsp black pepper
  • 1 clove garlic, minced (optional)
  • 1 green onion, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions:

1. Heat oil in a pan or wok over medium-high heat.
2. f using an egg, scramble it in the pan, then remove and set aside.
3. Add garlic (if using) and sauté for a few seconds. Then, add mixed vegetables and cook for 2-3 minutes.
4. Add the rice and stir-fry for another 2-3 minutes.
5. Pour in soy sauce, salt, and black pepper. Mix well.
6. If you set aside the egg, add it back now. Stir everything together.
7. Garnish with green onions and serve hot.

Mashed Potatoes

Ingredients:

  • 3 large potatoes (peeled and chopped)
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • ¼ cup milk (adjust as needed)
  • ½ tsp salt (or to taste)
  • ¼ tsp black pepper
  • 1 clove garlic, minced (optional, for extra flavor)
  • Fresh parsley or chives (for garnish)

Instructions:

1. Boil the potatoes in a pot of salted water for about 15-20 minutes or until fork-tender.
2. Drain the water and mash the potatoes while they are still hot.
3. Add butter, milk, salt, and black pepper. Mix well until smooth and creamy.
4. For extra flavor, add minced garlic while mashing.
5. Garnish with fresh parsley or chives and serve warm.

Baked Sweet Potatoes

Ingredients:

  • 2 medium sweet potatoes
  • 1 tbsp olive oil or melted butter
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ¼ tsp black pepper
  • Optional: honey, cinnamon, or chili flakes for extra flavor

Instructions:

1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
2. Wash and scrub the sweet potatoes, then pat them dry.
3. Pierce the potatoes with a fork a few times to let steam escape while baking.
4. Rub with olive oil or melted butter and sprinkle with salt and pepper.
5. Place them on a baking tray and bake for 40-50 minutes, or until they are soft and caramelized.
6. Slice open and add toppings like honey, cinnamon, or chili flakes if desired.
